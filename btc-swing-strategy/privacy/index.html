Privacy Policy

CohesivX BTC Monitor — Last updated: 07 June 2026

CohesivX BTC Monitor is an experimental Bitcoin structural monitoring application. The application does not require user registration, does not create user accounts, does not request personal identity information, does not execute financial transactions and does not manage user funds.

Data Collection

CohesivX BTC Monitor does not intentionally collect personal data from users. The application may use standard technical functionality required to display online content, including access to the internet, local cache, browser or WebView storage, and public market-related data sources.

The application does not request access to contacts, SMS, camera, microphone, precise location, photos, files or phone call data.

Financial Data

CohesivX BTC Monitor does not collect bank account data, wallet keys, seed phrases, exchange credentials, payment card data or other financial account information.

The application does not connect to user crypto wallets and does not perform buy, sell, deposit, withdrawal or trading actions.

Third-Party Data Sources

The application may display publicly available Bitcoin-related information, market data, structural indicators and interpretation generated by the CohesivX mechanism. Some data may be loaded from external public APIs or hosting services necessary for the normal operation of the application.

Purpose of the Application

CohesivX BTC Monitor is provided for informational and experimental purposes only. The information displayed by the application is not financial advice, investment advice, trading advice or a recommendation to buy, sell or hold Bitcoin or any other asset.

Data Sharing

CohesivX BTC Monitor does not sell user data. The application does not intentionally share personal user data with third parties.

Children

CohesivX BTC Monitor is not designed for children and is intended for a general audience interested in experimental market structure monitoring.

Security

The application is designed with minimal permissions and does not request access to sensitive device features.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be published on this page.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the developer through the official developer contact information listed on Google Play or through the CohesivX / Coeziv 3.14 Project.
